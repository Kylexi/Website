ABOUT

Welcome to my portfolio. I am, among many things, a data scientist. This term is vague due to the current AI bubble in tech, so let me clarify: I enjoy building/fine-tuning predictive models and working with raw data as a means to add value to businesses and individual lives. Generally, my interests lie in utilizing machine learning in novel ways, but I'm also partial to programming and computers as a whole.

Don't mistake me for that nerd who codes in the basement into the late hours and cannot interact with the bulk of society. I take pleasure in conveying and demonstrating complex ideas to groups of all backgrounds. Be it the colleague with a PhD in physics, the investor with economic know-how, or the five year old trying to satiate his curiosity, I strive to bring clarity to obfuscated concepts. Knowing how to apply machine learning and statistical models is only half the battle; the other half (which is becoming more and more important as data science itself becomes accessible to all) is convincing others why conclusions are so.
